### Welcome to "Bitcoin Boom: Empowering Boomers for a Brighter Legacy"

This interactive text-based learning game is tailored for Baby Boomers like you, who want to understand Bitcoin not just as "internet money," but as a tool to reshape the economic system for your children's (and grandchildren's) future. We'll revisit the flaws in the post-World War II fiat system that created inequality and affordability crises, then explore how Bitcoin offers solutions. You'll play as a Boomer "mentor" guiding your Millennial/Gen Z "protégé" through history and into a potential Bitcoin-powered future.

Inspired by *What Happened in 1971?*, we'll use real 2025 data, tables, and simple chart descriptions to make it clear and factual. Each level includes a story snippet, key data, and a quiz. Answer correctly for +10 Insight Points (start at 0). Wrong? Get a hint and retry. Aim for 60/60 to "unlock" a legacy-building action plan.

**Game Rules:**
- Read the narrative and data.

- **Inflation Surge Post-1971**: Average U.S. inflation was ~2% pre-1971; post-1971, it averaged 4%, peaking at 13.5% in 1980. By 2025, cumulative inflation has devalued the dollar by 85% since 1971.
- **Debt Explosion**: U.S. national debt was $398B in 1971 (~$3T adjusted); now over $35T in 2025, fueled by fiat printing.
