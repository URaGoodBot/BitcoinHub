### Improvements with Additional FRED Data
To elevate this from a basic gauge to a Buffett-grade moat-detector or Cowen-esque multi-indicator dashboard, layer in series that reveal velocity, policy signals, and derived irregularities. These expose how money moves (or hoards), not just swells—key for spotting inflation traps or crypto catalysts. Prioritize high-frequency for real-time edge.

Here's an expanded list of 5 high-value additions (with IDs, why they matter, and FRED links). They complement your core set by adding context (e.g., velocity drops signal stagnation, rates tie to Fed pivots).

| Series ID | Title | Frequency | Why It Adds Value for Irregularities | FRED Link |
|-----------|-------|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------|
| M2V | Velocity of M2 Money Stock | Quarterly | Shows money circulation speed; plunges (e.g., post-2020) signal hoarding/trapped liquidity, amplifying debasement risks without growth. Cross with M2 for full picture. | [View on FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/M2V) |
| M1V | Velocity of M1 Money Stock | Quarterly | Narrower focus on transaction money; divergences from M2V highlight credit freezes or digital shifts—Cowen would plot this vs. BTC for cycle turns. | [View on FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/M1V) |
| FEDFUNDS | Effective Federal Funds Rate | Monthly | Policy barometer; spikes/drains correlate with reserve crunches, distorting supply. Overlay with Reserves for irregularity alerts (e.g., >5% YoY signals tightening). | [View on FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/FEDFUNDS) |
| TREAST | U.S. Treasury Securities Held by Fed | Weekly | Balance sheet breakdown; surges indicate QE monetization, inflating base irregularly. Track vs. WALCL for asset mix shifts. | [View on FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/TREAST) |
| WSHOMCB | Mortgage-Backed Securities Held by Fed: Wednesday Level | Weekly | QE relic; runoffs (e.g., 2022+) drain liquidity subtly. Anomalies here presage housing/credit distortions—vital for macro like Saylor's fiat critique. | [View on FRED](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/WSHOMCB) |

**Derived Metrics for Extra Edge**:
- **Net Liquidity Proxy**: Calculate as WALCL (millions) / 1,000 - WTREGEN (millions) / 1,000 - RRPONTSYD. This "effective reserves" measure (popular in crypto circles) flags hidden floods/drains—e.g., low levels precede risk-off.
- **Excess Reserves**: WRESBAL (millions) / 1,000 - REQRESNS (billions; add series REQRESNS). Highlights surplus cash sloshing.
- **Debasement Ratio**: M2 / BOGMBASE (normalized to billions)—rising multiplier shows credit amplification irregularities.
