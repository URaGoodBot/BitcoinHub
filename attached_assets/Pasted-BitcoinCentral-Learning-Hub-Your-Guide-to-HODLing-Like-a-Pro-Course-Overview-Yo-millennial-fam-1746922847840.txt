Section 1: Introduction to Bitcoin 
Lesson 1: What Is Bitcoin, and Why Should I Care?
Duration: 30 mins
Description: Alright, let’s start with the basics—no snooze-fest history lesson here. Bitcoin’s like digital gold, created by some mysterious dude (or crew) named Satoshi Nakamoto. It’s money without banks, middlemen, or your nosy government tracking your late-night Taco Bell runs. We’ll cover:  
Why Bitcoin’s a big deal (spoiler: it’s anti-establishment AF).  

How it’s decentralized (no CEO to cancel you).  

The vibe of “number go up” and why people HODL.
Key Takeaways:  

Bitcoin = peer-to-peer digital cash.  

No bank, no problem—just math and code.  

It’s scarce (only 21 million coins ever, so no infinite printing like fiat).

Duration: 40 mins

How blocks and chains keep Bitcoin secure.  

Why “trustless” is a flex (no need to trust sketchy institutions).  

Mining, but not the pickaxe kind—how computers get paid to keep the network honest.
Key Takeaways:  

Blockchain = a tamper-proof ledger for all Bitcoin transactions.  

Miners are the real MVPs, solving math puzzles to secure the network.  

Decentralization means no single point of failure (suck it, banks).

Section 2: Bitcoin Transactions 
Lesson 4: Sending and Receiving Bitcoin: Yeet Those Sats
Duration: 45 mins
Description: Time to get hands-on. Sending Bitcoin’s easier than Venmo, but you gotta know the rules so you don’t yeet your BTC into the void. We’ll cover:  
Setting up a wallet (hot vs. cold, like your ex’s mood swings).  

Public and private keys (don’t share your private key, ever).  

How to send BTC without screwing it up (double-check that address, fam).
Key Takeaways:  

Wallets store your keys, not your Bitcoin (it lives on the blockchain).  

Always verify wallet addresses—typos are a one-way ticket to pain.  

Hardware wallets = the GOAT for keeping your BTC safe.

Mempool vibes—where transactions chill before getting confirmed.

Higher fees = faster confirmations (pay up or wait).

Lesson 6: Staying Safe: Don’t Get Rekt by Scams
Duration: 40 mins
Description: Crypto’s the Wild West, and scammers are out here DMing you like fake Tinder matches. We’ll teach you:  
How to spot phishing sites and fake wallets.  

Why “send me BTC, I’ll double it” is a lie (sorry, Elon imposters).  

Best practices for securing your stack (seed phrases, 2FA, don’t flex on X).
Key Takeaways:  

If it sounds too good to be true, it’s a scam. Period.  

Write down your seed phrase and hide it like it’s your diary.  

Use 2FA and a reputable exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, not ShadyMcCrypto.com).
